Mermaid School
 
 

The latest attraction, Mermaid School, provides children ages 7 – 12 years of age the opportunity to swim in the aquarium and be trained to swim like a Mermaid complete with their very own tails. Beginning with a warm-up session and concluding with a behind the scenes tour of the aquarium. This 90-minute program is $100 per child every Saturday and Sunday 8:30 a.m. – 10 a.m.
